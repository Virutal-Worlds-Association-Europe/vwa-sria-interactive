# 4c. Optics and Photonics

## Scope

Europe has strong leadership in optics and photonics technologies, which are fundamental enablers for XR displays, sensing systems, and communication infrastructure supporting Virtual Worlds.

## Key Research Areas

### Display Technologies
- Micro-LED and OLED displays
- Holographic displays
- Waveguide optics for AR
- Foveated rendering optics
- High-resolution, low-power displays

### Optical Components
- Compact optical systems for XR
- Diffractive and refractive optics
- Adaptive optics for vision correction
- Lightweight optical materials
- Manufacturing scalability

### Sensing and Imaging
- LiDAR and depth sensing
- Eye tracking optical systems
- Hand and gesture tracking optics
- Spatial mapping sensors
- Miniaturized imaging systems

### Photonic Communications
- Optical interconnects for data centers
- Free-space optical communications
- Silicon photonics integration
- High-bandwidth optical networks
- Energy-efficient optical transmission

## European Strengths

Europe has world-leading capabilities in:
- Precision optics manufacturing
- Photonics research and innovation
- Optical materials and coatings
- Industrial photonics applications
- Academic and industrial collaboration

## Strategic Recommendations

### For Policymakers
1. Support European photonics industry and R&D
2. Fund optics manufacturing infrastructure
3. Promote European standards for optical components
4. Facilitate academia-industry collaboration
5. Protect intellectual property in photonics

### For Industry
1. Invest in next-generation display technologies
2. Develop compact, efficient optical systems
3. Scale manufacturing of XR optical components
4. Collaborate on European supply chains
5. Pursue sustainable photonics solutions

### For Research Institutions
1. Advance micro-display technologies
2. Research novel optical architectures
3. Develop energy-efficient photonic devices
4. Prototype integrated optical systems
5. Transfer technology to European industry
