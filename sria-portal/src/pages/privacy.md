# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 2026**

## Introduction

The Virtual Worlds SRIA portal is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a transparent, GDPR-compliant experience. This privacy policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information.

## Our Commitment to Privacy

This website is designed with privacy as a core principle:

- **No cookies**: We do not use any cookies on this website
- **No tracking**: We do not track your browsing behavior
- **No analytics**: We do not use Google Analytics or any similar tracking tools
- **No third-party advertising**: We do not display any advertisements
- **No personal data collection**: We do not collect personal information through this website

## Information We May Collect

### Feedback Forms

If you choose to provide feedback through our Tally.so-embedded forms:

- Your submission is processed by Tally.so
- We only collect information you voluntarily provide
- This information is used solely to improve the SRIA portal
- Please review [Tally.so's Privacy Policy](https://tally.so/help/privacy-policy) for details on how they handle your data

### Server Logs

Like most websites, our hosting provider may collect standard server log information, including:

- IP addresses
- Browser type
- Access times
- Pages visited

This information is used solely for technical maintenance and security purposes.

## Data Storage and Security

- All content is served over secure HTTPS connections
- We do not maintain any user databases
- Feedback submissions are stored securely by Tally.so

## Your Rights Under GDPR

As this website does not collect or process personal data directly, most GDPR rights are not applicable. However, if you have submitted feedback:

- **Right to Access**: You can request information about data you've submitted
- **Right to Erasure**: You can request deletion of your feedback submissions
- **Right to Object**: You can object to processing of your data

To exercise these rights regarding feedback submissions, please contact us through the feedback form or reach out to the Virtual Worlds Association directly.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

## Third-Party Services

### Tally.so (Feedback Forms)

Our feedback forms are embedded from Tally.so. When you submit feedback:

- Your data is transmitted to and processed by Tally.so
- Tally.so is GDPR compliant
- Review their [Privacy Policy](https://tally.so/help/privacy-policy) for more details

## Contact Information

For questions about this privacy policy or data protection practices, please contact the Virtual Worlds Association through the official channels listed in the SRIA documentation.

---

**Note**: This is a privacy-first website. We believe in transparency and user autonomy. By not using cookies, tracking, or analytics, we ensure your browsing experience is completely private.
